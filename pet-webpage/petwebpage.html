Mia's Little Corner

About Mia

Mia is a rescue from a shelter in Missouri. She was adopted at 3 months old. Mia is a mix of standard schnauzer and skye terrier. She weighs about 45 lbs.

Mia's Favorite Things

  • staring out of windows
  • chasing squirrels (or any small animal, really)
  • chewing on her Nylabones
  • eating PB whenever she can get it

More Information for Pet Owners

Mia is protective and quite territorial. She likes people A LOT, but only if we all meet outside before coming in the house.
